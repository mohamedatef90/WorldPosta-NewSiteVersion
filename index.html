transfer data from your old email account to your new WorldPosta email account. You can access the migration service from the dashboard.


To migrate a single account, fill in the form with the needed source and destination information. The source information is the information for your old email account, and the destination information is the information for your new WorldPosta email account.


To migrate multiple accounts, you can download a sample CSV file and fill in the information for all of the accounts that you want to migrate. Then, upload the CSV file to WorldPosta.


Once you have started the migration process, WorldPosta will begin transferring the data from your old email account to your new WorldPosta email account. The migration process can take some time, depending on the amount of data that is being transferred.
