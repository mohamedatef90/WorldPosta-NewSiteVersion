Once you have started the migration process, WorldPosta will begin transferring the data from your old email account to your new WorldPosta email account. The migration process can take some time, depending on the amount of data that is being transferred.

SLAs are important for businesses that rely on cloud services because they can help to ensure that the services meet your needs. SLAs can also help to protect businesses from financial penalties if the provider fails to meet the agreed-upon level of service.

The uptime of a cloud service is the percentage of time that the service is available to users. A typical uptime guarantee for a cloud service is 99.9%.

The performance of a cloud service is the speed and responsiveness of the service. SLAs typically include metrics such as response time, throughput, and latency.
